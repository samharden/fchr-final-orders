This is a repository of the Final Orders issued by the Florida Commission on Human Relations.


The FCHR handles administrative claims of employment and housing discrimination. Before filing a discrimination claim in court, a person must exhaust their administrative remedies. This means they must typically file a claim with the FCHR first.


You can search or sort through the decisions. This site is build in NodeJS, serving JSON. To access this site as an API, click the top-left menu.
